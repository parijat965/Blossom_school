Our Programs

We offer a thoughtful curriculum tailored to each stage of a child’s early years—from playful learning in Nursery to strong academic foundations in Class 4.

Nursery

Focus on play, creativity, and sensory development in a caring and joyful environment.

Lower Kindergarten (LKG)

Introduction to letters, numbers, and social skills with playful learning.

Upper Kindergarten (UKG)

Building early literacy and numeracy skills with structured activities and games.

Class 1

Introduction to formal education with focus on core subjects and communication skills.

Class 2

Enhanced literacy, math, and activity-based science with values and teamwork.

Class 3 & 4

Strengthening academic concepts, reading, writing, and public speaking with creativity.
